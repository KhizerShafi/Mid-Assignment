Facebook

A leading social networking platform that allows users to connect with friends and family, share updates, photos, and videos, join groups, and discover trending content.

Instagram

A photo and video-sharing platform where users can upload, edit, and share visual content with their followers, explore posts from friends and celebrities, and engage with interactive features like Stories and Reels.

Twitter

A microblogging platform that enables users to share short text-based posts, known as "tweets," follow other users, join conversations through hashtags, and stay updated with news, trends, and events in real-time.

LinkedIn

A professional networking platform designed for professionals to connect, network, and engage with colleagues, recruiters, and industry experts, showcase their skills and experience, and explore job opportunities and business insights.

TikTok

A short-form video platform where users can create, share, and discover entertaining and engaging videos, explore trending challenges, music, and creative content, and connect with a global community of creators and viewers.

YouTube

A video-sharing platform where users can upload, watch, and share videos on a wide range of topics, including music, gaming, vlogs, tutorials, and more, subscribe to channels, engage with creators, and discover new content through personalized recommendations and trending lists.
